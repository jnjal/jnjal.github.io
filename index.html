Answers

Find answers to common questions about my design process, services etc...

What services do you provide?
I specialize in brand identity and packaging design, with experience in web design, UI/UX, and broader brand experiences.
How do I start working with you?
Simply book a free call or send an email. We will discuss your needs and see if we are a good fit.
What design tools do you use?
I primarily use the Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign) and Figma for digital products.
How long does a project take?
Timelines vary depending on scope, but a typical branding project takes 2-4 weeks.
Do you provide revisions?
Yes, I include 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure the final design meets your expectations.
